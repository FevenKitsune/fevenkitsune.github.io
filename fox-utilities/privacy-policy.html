Privacy Policy for Fox Utilities

We are committed to protecting your privacy while using our Discord bot. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect and use information within the bot's operation.

Information Collection and Use

Error Logging: To ensure the bot functions correctly, we log command errors for debugging purposes. These logs capture the specific command that caused the error but do not include your name, the server name, or any other personally identifiable information.

Data Persistence: Our bot does not use databases or other persistent storage mechanisms to retain user information. The exception is the error logging mentioned above, which is temporary and used solely for troubleshooting.

Misuse Policy: We strictly adhere to the principle of data minimization. We will never use the bot to access, collect, store, or share any information beyond what is explicitly described in this policy.

Data Security

While we do not store personally identifiable information, we take reasonable precautions to protect the error logs from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

Our bot may interact with third-party services (such as Discord's API) to provide functionality. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for their data practices.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the bot's Discord server or other appropriate channels.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to any error logs containing your commands. You can also request that we delete these logs. Please contact us using the information below.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or the bot's data practices, please contact us at our Discord server.

Effective Date: 7/18/2024

Important Notes:
This policy is in addition to, and does not replace, Discord's own privacy policy.
Our bot is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
